Our Courses

Comprehensive English Programs Based on Global Success Textbook Series

MOET-Approved Curriculum

We follow the Global Success Textbook Series prescribed by the Ministry of Education and Training (MOET) Vietnam. This internationally recognized curriculum is designed to progressively build English skills from beginner to upper-intermediate levels.

Little Kids Program

Age 3-5 years

Program Overview

Our Little Kids program introduces English through play, songs, stories, and interactive activities. We create a fun, nurturing environment where young learners develop a love for English naturally.

Learning Objectives

  • Basic vocabulary recognition (colors, numbers, animals, family)
  • Simple greetings and everyday expressions
  • Phonemic awareness and pronunciation
  • Listening comprehension through stories and songs
  • Confidence in speaking through games and activities

Teaching Methodology

  • Total Physical Response (TPR)
  • Storytelling and role-play
  • Songs, rhymes, and chants
  • Hands-on activities and crafts
  • Visual aids and flashcards
3 sessions/week
Max 8 students
45 minutes/session

Primary School Program

Grades 1-5

Program Overview

Building strong foundations in reading, writing, listening, and speaking using the Global Success textbook series aligned with MOET standards.

Learning Objectives

  • Reading comprehension and fluency
  • Basic grammar structures and sentence formation
  • Vocabulary expansion (500+ words per level)
  • Writing simple paragraphs and stories
  • Confident oral presentations
  • Listening skills for academic purposes

Course Content

  • Global Success textbooks (Grades 1-5)
  • Phonics and spelling patterns
  • Grammar: tenses, articles, prepositions
  • Reading: fiction and non-fiction texts
  • Writing: narratives, descriptions, letters
  • Speaking: dialogues, presentations, discussions
3 sessions/week
Max 10 students
60 minutes/session

Secondary School Program

Grades 6-9

Program Overview

Advanced English skills development preparing students for high school and international communication. Focus on academic English and real-world application.

Learning Objectives

  • Advanced grammar mastery
  • Academic writing (essays, reports, research)
  • Critical reading and analysis
  • Fluent spoken English for various contexts
  • Presentation and public speaking skills
  • Vocabulary for academic and social purposes (1000+ words per level)

Course Content

  • Global Success textbooks (Grades 6-9)
  • Complex grammar: conditionals, passive voice, reported speech
  • Essay writing and composition
  • Literature appreciation
  • Debate and discussion skills
  • Exam preparation techniques
  • Project-based learning
3 sessions/week
Max 10 students
90 minutes/session

High School Preparation

Grade 9+

Program Overview

Intensive preparation for high school English and international proficiency exams. Focus on advanced communication skills and academic excellence.

Learning Objectives

  • Upper-intermediate to advanced English proficiency
  • IELTS/TOEFL preparation foundations
  • Academic research and writing
  • Critical thinking and analysis
  • Professional presentation skills
  • English for specific purposes

Course Content

  • Advanced Global Success materials
  • Test-taking strategies
  • Academic writing and research methods
  • Literature analysis
  • Current events and discussions
  • Cross-cultural communication
3-4 sessions/week
Max 10 students
90 minutes/session
