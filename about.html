With years of experience in English language education, Mr. Bình specializes in teaching children from kindergarten through secondary school. His expertise spans:

  • Tiếng Anh mầm non (Kindergarten English)
  • Tiếng Anh tiểu học (Primary School English)

Mr. Bình's teaching philosophy centers on creating an engaging, understanding-based learning environment where students develop not just language skills, but confidence in using English for real-world communication.
